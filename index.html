Frequently Asked Questions

“Life without knowledge is death in disguise.”
- Talib Kweli

What is included with a rental?

A Boosted Board, Bluetooth remote, board and controller chargers, a safety helmet, and a safety tutorial on how to use everything.

What model are the boards?

All Boosted Boards the newest, higher end Gen2 Dual+ Boosted Boards.

Where can I ride a Boosted Board?

Boosted Boards are approved to be ridden anywhere on UGA’s campus. You can take them on sidewalks or anywhere that a bike would be ridden.

Where do I get the Boosted Board when I rent one?

We meet you anywhere on campus that is easiest for you.

How far can these boards go?

On full charge, the range is 6 – 7 miles, depending on ride conditions.

How long does it take to charge?

A Boosted Board charges up 85% in 30 minutes, and 100% in just over an hour. Perfect for riding, charging, riding, charging, riding…

Are these boards safe?

Yes! So long as you wear our helmets and ride carefully! We wouldn’t rent them out if they weren’t safe though. We teach you the proper way to ride, and we provide a helmet to help keep you safe. The controller has different modes, and you can ride in Beginner mode to limit maximum speed to a slow, safe level. It’s perfect for learning to ride!

Is there a limit on how much I can ride in one day?

Absolutely not! We provide you with a charger to keep your board juiced up for a full day of fun!
